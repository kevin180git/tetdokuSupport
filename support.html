Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Tetdoku?

Tetdoku combines Tetris-style block placement with Sudoku logic. Place the falling blocks to complete rows while ensuring no numbers repeat in any row, column, or designated region.

Is Tetdoku free to play?

Yes! Tetdoku is free to download and play with optional in-app purchases for additional features and content.

What devices are supported?

Tetdoku is available for iOS and Android devices. Check the App Store or Google Play Store for specific compatibility requirements.
